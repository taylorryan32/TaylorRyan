About Matt & coaching philosophy

Matt believes great pickleball is built on repeatable fundamentals and smart decision-making—not tricks that break under pressure. His coaching blends video feedback when helpful, purposeful drilling, and live-ball scenarios so skills transfer to real matches.

What clients improve

  • ✓ Strategy — patterns, shot selection, and court awareness
  • ✓ Footwork — split-step timing, balance, and recovery steps
  • ✓ Consistency — repeatable strokes under mild pressure
  • ✓ Serve & return — depth, disguise, and neutralizing
  • ✓ Net play — dinks, resets, and hands battles
  • ✓ Mental game — routines between points and composure
